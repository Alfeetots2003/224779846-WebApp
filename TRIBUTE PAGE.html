Betty Doclan Bangtowan

1944-2024

"'Kasiyan na' which means 'Everything will be okey.'"

Here is the timeline of Betty Doclan Bangtowan:

1944 - Betty was born on November 3, 1944. She was named by her parents with her ancestor's name "Ban-Ay".

1961 - Betty and Alfonso Bangtowan has been living together as husband and wife. And they've been lived together at Poblacion, Tuba, Benguet.

1990 - When Betty and Alfonso both decided to get married and resided at San Vicente Baguio City.

1962-1983 - Where Betty and Alfonso was blessed by 8 childrens named: Jerry, Julie, Jocelyn, Juluis, Leilani, Lorenzo, Evangeline and Placido.

1996 - When Betty's husband, Alfonso died due to cardiac arrest. She continued to live and worked day and night just to provide for her family as she raised her children alone. She worked hard and managed all the lots near their village.

Luckily, all of her children got a job and family and 3 of them were able to join and became a Public servants. Betty was also blessed by many grand daughters and sons.

2010 - When Lorenzo, Betty's 6th child at the age of 32 died due to cardiac arrest. Her cousin Toy-ok also died 3 days after her son's death.

2020 - Betty's second child, Julie died due to cardiac arrest as well.

2024 - March 05, 2024. At the age of 80, Betty decided to rest.
